# Overall Score

## Purpose

Combine all scoring engines into a single project score.

---

## Inputs

Coverage Score

Quality Score

Risk Score

Confidence

---

## Output

Overall Score

Overall Rating

---

## Ratings

95-100

Excellent

90-94

Very Good

80-89

Good

70-79

Needs Improvement

Below 70

Poor

---

## Principles

Never allow one metric to dominate the overall score.

Security issues have higher impact.

Coverage and Quality have equal importance.
